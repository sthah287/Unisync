Services We Provide:

Learning Resources

Tailored to your Major and preference, we provide you all the online learning resources you'll need to have a constant high GPA across four years. A one-stop platform for all your reading as well as skill development(for tech students).

Discussion Forums

Stuck in a problem or got a query about coursework, you can post here. ChatGPT ain't university specific, so discuss your university related problems here and hopefully get it solved by the help of your friends, seniors or professors.

Peer Mentoring

Feeling like you need a personal guidance on some topics you are being confused? Choose from a list of mentors who are ready to tutor you passionately. Connect with them via linkedin or gmail and book their available hours.

About Us

This project is a prototype on an web application which ultimate vision is to help students with their academic goals. We provide online course resources, an active discussion forum to help solve all your problems, and lastly a peer mentoring feature for students who are struggling.

For now, we are targeting only university students from Fisk and TSU. But as we expand, we can and are hoping to launch this idea on a bigger scale and to other peoples too.

For now, you are required to have .edu email to be able to create an account in this project. You will be then able to access all the features as well as track your progress on this project.

All of the contributors are freshmans from Fisk Univeristy who are passionate on everything about tech stuffs.
